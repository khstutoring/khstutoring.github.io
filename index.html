ABOUT ME

KATIE HUSMANN STEVENSON

Founder of KHS Tutoring Services

Thanks for checking out my website! I love tutoring, and it shows in my work. The one-on-one format gives me the freedom to cut through to the heart of things. I’m continually driven by questions like “What does this particular student really need?” and “How can I provide that for them?”

Questions like that led me to develop two of the services I now offer routinely: Academic Life Skills Accountability and Foundational Writing Skills. I developed each of these programs over several years by listening to common parent requests and observing student learning patterns. I describe each of these services in more detail in my orientation packet, which you can request via email below.

In addition to the services I designed myself, I got my initial Specialized Reading Remediation training working at the therapeutic learning center Lindamood-Bell Learning Processes. Similarly, I owe my success at Subject Tutoring in English and Spanish to the excellent education I received at Wilcox High School, where I was valedictorian, and Wheaton College, where I earned my major in English Writing.

If you’re interested in a Free Assessment, or just want to get to know me better, please don’t hesitate to send me an email and we can set up a Skype appointment.

Wishing you all the best,

Katie
